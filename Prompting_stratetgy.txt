## Prompting Strategy

### Upload Page
- **Optional Context Textarea:**
  - Placeholder: `Enter optional context here...`
- **File Selection:**
  - Label: `Select Images:`
  - Instructions: `Choose multiple images (PNG, JPG, JPEG) to analyze.`
- **Submit Button:**
  - Label: `Describe Features`

### Results Page
- **Headline:**
  - `Image Descriptions`
- **Instructions:**
  - `Below are the detailed descriptions and feature analyses of your uploaded images.`

### Error Messages
- **Text Extraction Error:**
  - `Error: No text extracted from the image. Please ensure the image is clear and readable.`
- **Feature Identification Error:**
  - `Feature not identified. The text extracted may not match any known features.`
